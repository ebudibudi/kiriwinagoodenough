Kiriwina Goodenough Scholarship Statement of Values

The Kiriwina Goodenough Scholarship Program (KGSP) is an establishment through the initiative of the Open Member then approved and endorsed by the District Development Authority (DDA) to setup this program and fund it through the District Services Improvement Program (DSIP) Funding. It is a public funding therefore the citizens of Kiriwina Goodenough Electorate are the direct recipients of this funding. It would be proper to have a fair Distribution of Sponsorship and the Recipients of this funding must take Pride and total Responsibilities for maximum benefit since it is a privilege.
