Kiriwina-Goodenough

Kiriwina Goodenough has immense potential in agriculture, fishery and tourism. Many of these potentials have been identified under the National Government’s Economic Corridor Planning Initiative which provides the framework for achieving Vision 2050 for PNG to become a prosperous middle income country.

Under the Economic sector, there are various divisions that operate in the district. Some of the active examples are Agriculture, livestock & Food security division, Tourism division, Lands division, SMEs etc...

Subsistence agriculture dominates the agricultural landscape by 80 % of the smallholder farmers. The common produces are copra and cocoa on Goodenough Island.The fresh vegetables and local staples for food and income generation are produces in large quantities on both Kiriwina and Goodenough.
